Traditional NAS relied on expert-designed search spaces and strategies

Traditional NAS depends on manually designed search spaces and strategies, making it expert-driven and inflexible. The need for repeated training and evaluation leads to high computational cost and slow convergence, while limited adaptability makes it difficult to handle diverse real-world requirements such as accuracy, efficiency, and deployment constraints.
